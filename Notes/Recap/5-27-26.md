The party set out from [[Prigwort]] to find the kidnaped daughter of the [[Elevated Wilfrinda Grump]]. She is thought to have been taken by some malicious faires and whisked off to [[Drooman Knoll]]. As you all approached the low flat-topped hill you heard sad keening pipes interspersed with harsh and raucous laughter. You discovered the ruined keep at the top of the hill contained  a group of [[Crookhorns]], [[Breggles]] that have been mutated by some unknow and evil force. 

You were able to quietly take out the guard standing watch. But when you engaged the Crookhorns head on you found them quite a challenge. Shuler's character was run though by the horns of one and a second is attacking the rest of the party. What would you like to do?
